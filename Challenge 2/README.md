Company Sales Records Analysis
##Project Overview
This project analyzes company sales records using SQL, SQLite, and Python to evaluate customer interactions, sales performance, and credit-related business data.
The analysis combines multiple relational datasets to uncover insights that support:
customer relationship management,
sales tracking,
and business decision-making.

##Business Problem
The company needs a centralized way to analyze:
customer information,
sales personnel activity,
customer encounters,
and credit rating records.

The goal is to better understand customer engagement patterns and improve sales performance analysis using relational data.
