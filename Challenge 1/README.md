Car Sales Revenue Analysis
##Project Overview
This project analyzes automobile sales data using SQL, SQLite, and Python to uncover insights related to vehicle distribution, pricing, and regional revenue performance.
The analysis focuses on:
revenue generation by region,
top-performing vehicles,
pricing analysis,
and comparative sales performance across markets.

##Business Questions
The project answers the following questions:
How many unique cars originate from the USA?
What is the combined number of unique cars from Europe and Japan?
Which is the most expensive car?
Which car generates the highest revenue?
Which region generates the most revenue?
What is the revenue gap between the highest and lowest performing cars?

##Key Insights
Revenue performance varied significantly across regions.
Certain car models generated substantially higher revenue than others.
High-value vehicles were concentrated within specific regions.
Regional revenue analysis revealed clear differences in market performance.
